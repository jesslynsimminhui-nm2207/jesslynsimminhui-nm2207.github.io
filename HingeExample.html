Disclaimer and Limitations

Out of the profiles that like you and the profiles you've browsed, it does not differentiate the type of 'block' you've given. However, all the likes recorded are given by your profile. The matches can be differentiated between people who have matched you (reciprocated likes) and matches you've accepted. Finally, the radar chart and toggles will max out at 10 and 100% respectively. Were the stats be too polarised, it will be reflected as the maximum.
